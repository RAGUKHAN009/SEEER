2. HOW TO JOIN SEEER
Registration is easy. Simply select your reader from among our hundreds of gifted psychic advisors, click to chat, and you'll be walked through the registration process. You'll be asked to create a username and password. Once you're set up, you can start chatting immediately!
3. PAYMENT OPTIONS
PayPal is a safe and easy way to pay online. Simply select the amount you wish to add and click "Checkout with PayPal." You'll be redirected to the PayPal website to complete the funds transfer. When the transfer is complete, you'll be returned to Seeer to start your session.
